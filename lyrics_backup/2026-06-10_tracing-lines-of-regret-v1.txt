[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I trace the shadows falling cross your face
A midnight whisper in a quiet place
I pull you closer just to feel your breath
And leave behind the lines of my regret

[Verse 1]
The coffee cools but there is fire here
A burning ember when I hold you near
We watch the raindrops falling on the glass
I want this slow and tender night to last

[Pre-Chorus]
And every time you look into my eyes
The heavy burdens of the morning rise
But in this moment, there is only us
A quiet surrender to a burning trust

[Chorus]
So let me trace the lines of my regret
Upon your skin so I will not forget
I trade my sorrows for the way you move
And find redemption in the shape of you

[Post-Chorus]
Tracing lines, tracing every line with you
[Echo] (The shape of you)

[Verse 2]
Your fingertips are pulling at my sleeve
You ask me softly if I have to leave
I map the silver moonlight on your floor
And promise I could never ask for more

[Pre-Chorus 2]
And every time you look into my eyes
The heavy burdens of the morning rise
But in this moment, there is only us
A quiet surrender to a burning trust

[Chorus]
So let me trace the lines of my regret
Upon your skin so I will not forget
I trade my sorrows for the way you move
And find redemption in the shape of you

[Post-Chorus 2]
Tracing lines, tracing every line with you
[Echo] (The shape of you)

[Bridge]
So let me trace the lines of my regret
The shadows fall and all the memories fade
But in the warmth of every choice I made
I pull you closer, never letting go
It takes a night like this for Saeed to know
That this is where the truest feelings show

[Spoken]
I will hold you until the morning light
And keep you safe inside this quiet night

[Final Chorus]
So let me trace the lines of my regret
Upon your skin so I will not forget
I trade my sorrows for the way you move
And find redemption in the shape of you
